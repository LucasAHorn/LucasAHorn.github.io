About Me

Hello! I'm currently a sophomore at Iowa State University, where I'm pursuing a Bachelor's in Software Engineering. My journey into programming started when I was young, thanks to my dad who helped me dive into coding. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a passion, especially after taking Python and Java classes in high school. Now at Iowa State, I'm not only excelling in my coursework but also meeting incredible people who inspire me every day. Whether it's working on a project or exploring new technologies, I'm always excited to find creative solutions and push my boundaries.
